Residential

Aqua-Rex is the water softener alternative for homes, businesses, and swimming pools.

What Aqua-Rex Does

Prevents Scaling

It stops scale sticking to surfaces so heaters don’t scale up and shower heads stay clean.

Cleans Plumbing

It breaks down and cleans out the existing scale in a plumbing system.

Partially Softens

It partially softens hot water so soaps lather better for laundry and washing.
